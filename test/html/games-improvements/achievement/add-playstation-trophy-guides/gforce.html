Road Map

Overview:
-Estimated Trophy difficulty: 4.72/10
Offline: 44 (28 , 12 , 3 , 1 )
Online: 0
Approximate amount of time to Platinum: About 19 hours if two playthroughs only:
- 12 hours on your first playthrough going for the Master Agent trophy, plus about 7 more
hours on your second playthrough. Possible you will need a third playthrough if you miss
the Master Agent trophy, so be very careful when going for that trophy.
-Minimum number of playthroughs needed: 2
-Missable trophies: It's possible to miss the Master Agent trophy even when you are going for it if you miss even a few rooms so be careful.
-Do Cheats Disable Trophies?: Yes

 
Walkthrough:

You're going to have to play through the game twice, so you need to focus on a few trophies each time.

First Playthrough:
On your first playthrough play on either Easy or Normal, take your time and go for the Master Agent trophy, collect all 50 Silver Data Discs , and the 80% accuracy trophy .

You should also try to get all the " 100 Kills with Weapons " trophies on this first playthrough. None of them are overly difficult, just try to use them often enough and you will usually get ammo for the weapon you are using out of crates/boxes (do not buy ammo with your chips as this will most likely prevent you from having enough chips for the Master Agent trophy). Be sure you buy weapons as soon as they are available in the game so you can get the 100 kills with them and then move on to new weapons as soon as possible or back to a weapon you feel more comfortable with so you can more easily get the 80% accuracy trophy. Remember to let the computers that spit out little spider monsters keep spitting them out before you kill the computer as you can get a lot of kills on the spiders. If you do miss any of the 100, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough, but remember you will be rushing through on your second playthrough and the enemies will be harder on the hardest difficulty. So since you are taking your time so much on this first playthrough, it makes sense to just get all these out of the way so you don't need to worry about them on your second playthrough.

Second Playthrough:
If done correctly and you didn't miss any of the important trophies (like Master Agent, if you missed Master Agent you will have to play the game 3 times), on your second playthrough you should only need to get Distinguished Service Medal and G-Force Rapid Response Agent trophies, plus any weapon kill trophies, if you missed any.

Start your second playthrough on Special Agent difficulty and you will need to go for the " Beating the game in under 8 hours " trophy. It may seem more difficult to go through that fast on the hardest difficulty, but you will know what needs to be done in every area since you have already played through once. Spend your chips on ammo if you need it. You will get all the guns by getting the Gold Data Discs which can't be missed, but you will probably miss a good bit of the Silver Data Discs, however that shouldn't be a problem because you don't really need all the upgrades they provide in this playthrough.

If for some reason you missed the 80% accuracy trophy in your first playthrough you will need to get it in this playthrough, and since you will be rushing through it may be more difficult to take your time and aim on enemies carefully, so do try to be extra careful for that trophy on your first playthrough.

G-Force Trophy Guide

44 trophies ( 1  3  12  28  16  )

  • 100% Complete!

     

    You have unlocked every other trophy

  • Distinguished Service Medal

     

    Completed game twice

    (1)

    You will have to play the game a second time anyways to beat the game on Special Agent difficulty which unlocks after you beat the game once on any difficulty.

  • G-Force Rapid Response Agent

     

    Completed game in under 8 hours (on any difficulty setting)

    (1)

    Do not go for this and Master Agent on the same playthrough. To get this try to run past as many enemies as possible, unless of course you have to beat the enemies for the next door to open. If you take awhile to beat a certain area reload the last checkpoint and try again (of course remember to manually save each checkpoint when you get one also).

  • Silver Data Disk Discoverer

     

    Collected 50% (25) of all Silver Data Disks in the game

    (1)

    See "Silver Data Disk Expert"

  • Silver Data Disk Expert

     

    Collected 100% (50) of all Silver Data Disks in the game

    See this guide by erod550 at xbox360achievements.org for a complete walkthrough of all 50 data disc locations. Remember that you have to get all 50 silver discs to be able to buy everything for the Master Agent trophy so don't miss one.

  • Big Spender

     

    Spent more than 10000 SaberSense chips at kiosks.

    You will get this as you are going for the Master Agent trophy. Simply buy stuff (ammo capacity and character upgrades only if going for Master Agent) and you will get this trophy fairly early on in the game.

  • Wealthy Agent

     

    Accumulate more than 5000 SaberSense chips

    You need to have 5,000 chips on you at one time, so the easiest time to do this is at the start of the game. Simply don't buy anything until you get the trophy.

  • Tooled-Up Agent

     

    Purchased all new weapons and weapon upgrades (excluding ammo capacity)

    You will get this after you collect the last gold data disc and buy the item from the vending kiosk if you have already bought all the previousweapons unlocked through gold data discs. You cannot miss gold data discs in the game.

  • Master Agent

     

    Fully upgraded character with all possible weapons and upgrades

    (1)

    This is one of the hardest trophies in the game. You need to be very thorough and search everywhere. Always send Mooch up into vents you can't reach, check up on the hanging lights in rooms, try not to skip any areas. For instance there is an area where Speckles will tell you he thinks he knows a shortcut, and if you choose not to go that way the door will not open when you come back and you will not be able to get the chips from the enemies and boxes in that area when you come back.

    There are chips in boxes in the training level, don't miss those.

    Make sure you kill every enemy, don't skip any. This includes the satellite dishes that just block your path with a red wall and every enemy that drops chips. The only one that doesn't drop chips is the small spiders that slow you down. If you kill an enemy that is far away send Mooch to grab the chips or try to pull it in by holding R1.

    Sometimes when you die and there are chips you haven't collected from enemies they will not be there when you respwn. I'm not sure but this only happened sometimes, so just be careful to try to always grab chips as soon as you beat an enemy even if you're still in the middle of a fight.

    *Here is an alternative way to getting the Master Agent trophy. Thanks to Roughdawg4 for submitting this tip:

    "I noticed in my Super Agent game that when I used plasma like crazy, it dropped plasma. So I thought, what if I didn't use any guns and only my whip so my ammo was always full. Well since I don't use ammo (most of the time), I get nothing but chip drops. I should easily have 40,000 by the end of the game and no extra farming is necessary, just try to kill everything with your whip.

    These are the only things I didn't kill with my whip (forgive me I don't know names):
    The Fans
    The machine with the shield and laser
    And the 2 big flying machines (one shoots projectiles and the other missles).
    Everything else use your whip. And remember you have 10 lives on easy mode so you can afford to die a few times."

    One note if you do decide to go this way: Make sure that you are extra careful when you do use your guns as you should be going for the 80% accuracy trophy also, and if you are mostly using your whip you will need to be extra careful to miss as few shots as possible with your guns. Remember, 80% is 8 out 10 shots that need to hit, so you do need to be as careful as possible.

  • Plasma Gun Expert

     

    Defeated 100 enemies with Plasma Gun

    The Plasma Gun is one of your starting weapons and you will get this trophy just playing through game and early in the game. However, if for some odd reason you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • Cluster Rifle Expert

     

    Defeated 100 enemies with Cluster Rifle

    The Cluster Rifle is a fast shooting gun. It's the second gun you get in the game so you will have it early on and it holds alot of ammo. If you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • Flame Thrower Expert

     

    Defeated 100 enemies with Flame Thrower

    (1)

    The Flame Thrower is one of the last weapons you get in the game. Make sure to use it on the spiders that slow you down that the computers throw out at you, and don't kill the computer until you have run out of Flame Thrower ammo. Always use the ammo when you have it and you should have this on your first playthrough. If however you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • Freeze Gun Expert

     

    Defeated 100 enemies frozen by the Freeze Gun

    (1)

    Once you've unlocked and bought the Freeze Gun just freeze enemies and then kill them with the Electro Whip. Some of the easiest enemies to freeze are any of the small, crawling enemies, like the spiders sent out by the computers. If you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • Shot Bolter Expert

     

    Defeated 100 enemies with Shot Bolter

    The Shot Bolter is very effective against small flying enemies like the Air Fresheners and Shavers. This is one of the best weapons in the game so you shouldn't have a problem getting this trophy. If you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • Incendiary Charge Grenadier

     

    Defeated 50 enemies with Incendiary Charges

    You get to use Incendiary Charges by attacking the Incendiary Charge distributers that are in the game. When you are near one be sure to try to kill enemies close by with a charge as there aren't many of these throughout the game. There is a part in the game where you can continuoulsy respawn a Fire Extinguisher that is very close by to the charges. Activate the Fire Extinguisher, kill it with a charge, then repeat as many times as the Fire Extinguisher will respawn. Eventually it will stop spawning (after about 20 times). But that is still 20 free kills with the charges and some extra chips if going for the Master Agent trophy. (Note: Once the Fire Extinguisher quits spawning you will need to melt some ice to progress, you can just throw a charge at it to melt it since the Fire Extinguisher isn't spawning anymore.) If you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • RDV Pulse Cannon Expert

     

    Defeated 50 enemies with RDV Pulse Cannon

    Fire R1 while in the driving missions to kill enemies and get this trophy. If you don't have this after the last driving mission (there are only two) save in a new spot and load the save that starts the driving mission level and keep doing that until you get this trophy.

  • RDV Homing Missile Expert

     

    Defeated 50 enemies with RDV Homing Missiles

    Fire L1 to target an enemy and then again to kill the enemy for this trophy. If you don't have this after the last driving mission (there are only two) save in a new spot and load the save that starts the driving mission level and keep doing that until you get this trophy.

  • NanoHack Expert

     

    Defeated 50 enemies using NanoHacked enemies

    Some good enemies to Nanohack are the flying ones when there are smaller enemies on the ground. If you just remember to use this whenever you have ammo for it it shouldn't be a problem getting this on your first playthrough. However if you don't get the 100 kills in your first playthrough, the kills will carry over to your second playthrough.

  • Sharp Shooter

     

    Achieved an average hit rate of at least 80% with projectile weapons

    Make sure to take your time when attacking with projectile weapons. Always attack the satellite dishes that put up a red wall blocking your path with a gun based weapon (not the whip) as they don't move and so are very easy targets.

    The Shot Bolter is good against smaller flying enemies like the Electric Shavers and the Air Fresheners since it has a wide range and even if you don't hit directly on the enemy, if you're close it will most likely still hit since this weapon has a good spread.

  • Marching Agent

     

    Walk more than 20km with Darwin

    You should have this by the end of the game, but if you don't just walk around in the second to last level until you do unlock it. (In the very last level you will not be able to play as Darwin, so you need to this before you start the last level.)

  • G-Force Wings

     

    Fly more than 2km with Mooch

    You should have this by the end of the game, but if you don't just fly around in the last level until you do unlock it.

  • Intelligence Expert

     

    Scan 100 items in the game

    (1)

    This means 100 unique items. Make sure to scan every new enemy you encounter, all doors (locked/unlocked, wood, etc.), all types of boxes, special game items like silver and gold data discs, keycard, and anything you have to pick up in order to progress, all types of ammo and health, etc. If you do this from the start of the game you should have this midway through if you don't miss much.

  • G-Force Trainee

     

    Completed game on Easy difficulty setting

    See "G-Force Agent"

  • G-Force Recruit

     

    Completed game on Normal difficulty setting

    See "G-Force Agent"

  • G-Force Agent

     

    Completed game on Challenging difficulty setting

    Beat the game on Challenging. These trophies stack so if you play through on Challenging first you will also unlock beating the game on Easy and Normal. If on your first playthrough you play on Easy or Normal you will unlock this trophy when you complete your second playthrough on Special Agent difficulty.

 

Secret trophies

  • Mission Accomplished: "Snowball Unleashed"

     

    Progression

    After you free Blaster out of the elevator a cutscene will play and the trophy will unlock.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Countdown"

     

    Progression

    This will unlock after you infiltrate Saberling's office and have to send Mooch into the area with Saberling and a woman to retrieve a key card. Have Mooch give the key card back to Darwin and have Darwin use the key card on the computer. A cutscene will happen and the trophy will unlock.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Saberling Offices"

     

    Progression

    After you encounter a CD Player enemy you will need to go in the elevator (you need a key card to get in the elevator, the key card is located in a nearby room). Once you are in the elevator and it is heading down the trophy will unlock.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Central Computer"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock as you progress through the story after you beat a monster you will find out the virus didn't work and the trophy will unlock.

  • Mission Accomplished: "RDV Retreat"

     

    Progression

    This is a very short level, about 1-2 minutes long. You have to keep driving through the level, once you reach the end a cutscene will happen and you will get the trophy.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Distribution Center"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock after you shut down both shuttles and you take the lift to the next area.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Assembly Lines"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock after stopping the plant from making more appliances. Head towards the exit and get on the lift and the trophy will unlock soon after.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Chip Manufacture"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock after you destroy the two machines that are making the Saberling chips.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Reactor"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock as you head to the exit point after you destroy the reactor.

  • Mission Accomplished: "RDV Escape"

     

    Progression

    Like the other RDV mission this is a very short level, about 3-4 minutes long. You have to keep driving through the level, once you reach the end a cutscene will happen and you will get the trophy.

    *If you didn't get the trophies for 50 kills with RDV Pulse Cannon and RDV Homing Missile then be sure to save in a new save spot and then reload the save you had that was at the start of this mission to get those two weapon trophies as this is the last RDV mission in the game.

  • Mission Accomplished: "FBI Infiltration"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock after you enter the elevator you activated with the motion sensor laser.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Locate the Real Saber"

     

    Progression

    This trophy will unlock after you connect the infra-red equipment to the security system in the story.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Rescue Saber"

     

    Progression

    There is a big fight on top of the FBI Headquaters where Yancho is continuously talking to you over a loudspeaker and alot of enemies will come at you in sets. Once you finally beat the last ones you will save Saber and a cutscene will happen, also unlocking this trophy.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Clusterstorm"

     

    Progression

    After making your way to the end of the last level with Mooch and beating Clusterstorm's core system you will unlock this trophy and the ending cutscene of the game.

  • Mission Accomplished: "Satellite Menace"

     

    Progression

    This is the second to last level of the game, but this is the last level that you will play as Darwin so if you don't have the "Marching Agent: Walk more than 20km with Darwin" trophy make sure to walk around some and get it before completing this mission.

  • G-Force Special Agent

     

    Completed game on Special Agent difficulty setting

    This difficulty will unlock once you beat the game on Easy, Normal, or Challenging difficulty. You then need to beat the game on Special Agent difficulty to get this trophy.
